Fridoumi is an AI-powered cooking assistant that helps you manage your fridge ingredients and recommends recipes based on what you have on hand. It helps you reduce food waste and cook smarter every day.
We automatically analyze your registered ingredients and suggest the best matching recipes using AI. Free users receive a limited number of suggestions per day. Pro subscribers enjoy unlimited recommendations with no cap.
Open the Receipt Scanner screen, select or take a photo of your grocery receipt, and Fridoumi will automatically recognize the ingredients and add them to your pantry. Receipt scanning is a Pro subscriber feature.
Make sure the receipt photo is clear, well-lit, and not blurry. Shooting in a brighter environment significantly improves recognition accuracy. If the problem persists, please contact us at yoonseok84@gmail.com.
Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Fridoumi → Cancel Subscription. Your Pro access continues until the end of the current billing period. Fridoumi does not handle cancellations directly — all subscriptions are managed through Apple.
Pro includes: unlimited AI recipe suggestions, receipt scanning, smart shopping list, iCloud sync across all your Apple devices, and an ad-free experience. Available as monthly ($3.99/mo) or annual ($29.99/yr, saving 37%).
All your pantry data and recipes are stored locally on your device using SwiftData. Pro subscribers can optionally enable iCloud sync, which stores data in Apple's encrypted iCloud. Fridoumi does not operate its own servers.
We follow Apple's standard refund policy. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with your Apple ID.
When you first launch Fridoumi, you'll be prompted to allow notifications. Once granted, you'll automatically receive alerts 3 days before any ingredient's expiration date. You can adjust notification settings in iPhone Settings → Notifications → Fridoumi.
All local data stored on your device is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app. iCloud data (for Pro subscribers) is managed separately — to delete it, go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Storage → Fridoumi → Delete Data.
Your ingredient data is sent to our AI service provider solely to generate recipe recommendations. Receipt images (Pro) are sent for analysis and discarded immediately after processing. We do not sell your data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
